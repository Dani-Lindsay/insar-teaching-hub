> **Scholarships are like sunscreen — apply liberally.**

Apply for everything you are eligible for, early and often. The worst outcome is a rejection; the best is funding for fieldwork, a conference trip, or a research visit that changes the direction of your masters.

## Short courses and summer schools

### InSAR Processing and Analysis with ISCE (EarthScope)

A 5-day remote short course covering InSAR theory and processing methods from NASA/JPL, using the ISCE3 processing framework.

- **Applications:** Due ~June 9 each year
- **Cost:** Free
- **Link:** [https://www.earthscope.org/event/2026-technical-course-insar-processing-and-analysis-isce/](https://www.earthscope.org/event/2026-technical-course-insar-processing-and-analysis-isce/)

This course is highly recommended — it covers the theory and methods behind the software you will use throughout your research. Apply as early in your masters as possible.

If you miss the application window, past year materials are publicly available:
- 2025 recordings: [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLGQwSTwiUcKyFTPhELEVOjqzq9rPUJM1f)
- 2025 notebooks: [GitHub](https://github.com/isceplus/2025-isceplus)

### CLaSH Workshop

The CLaSH (Cascadia and Hikurangi) workshop brings together early-career researchers working on subduction zone science across the Pacific. Worth watching for — applications typically open in spring for a US summer workshop.

- **Stay informed:** Sign up for the CLaSH mailing list at [https://clash.vuw.ac.nz/](https://clash.vuw.ac.nz/)

### SZ4D and Cascadia Early-Career Activities

Both [SZ4D](https://www.sz4d.org/) and [Cascadia Quakes](https://cascadiaquakes.org/) have graduate-student-led activities and networks — good for connecting with international peers working on similar problems.

## Scholarships

### Fulbright New Zealand

Spend a few months at a leading international institution as part of your masters research. Very well regarded, and a genuine opportunity to build international connections early.

- **Deadline:** October 1 each year
- **Link:** [https://fulbright.org.nz/study-in-the-united-states/](https://fulbright.org.nz/study-in-the-united-states/)
- **Note:** You can defer a year if timing doesn't align with your research — worth applying even if your plans are not fully formed.

### Freemasons University Scholarships

New Zealand-based scholarship open to university students. Watch the deadline — it falls earlier in the year than most.

- **Link:** [https://freemasonsnz.org/freemasons-university-scholarships-2](https://freemasonsnz.org/freemasons-university-scholarships-2)

## Conferences

### International

| Conference | Frequency | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| **AGU Fall Meeting** | Annual (December, San Francisco) | The largest geoscience meeting in the world. Abstract deadline typically August. Apply for travel grants early — they go fast. |
| **EGU General Assembly** | Annual (April/May, Vienna) | European equivalent of AGU — strong InSAR and geodesy sessions. PICO format works well for early-career work. |
| **Fringe Workshop** | Every ~2 years (ESA) | InSAR-specific workshop run by ESA. Excellent for meeting the algorithm developers whose papers you are reading. |
| **IGARSS** | Annual | IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing — more technical/engineering focus, good for SAR methods. |

### New Zealand / Regional

| Conference | Frequency | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| **NZ Geophysics Society** | Annual | A good first conference — tight-knit community, supportive atmosphere for student presentations. |
| **GeoNZ / Geological Society of NZ** | Annual | Broader geology audience, useful for communicating InSAR to non-specialists. |

## Mailing lists to subscribe to

Announcements about summer schools, workshops, and funding often circulate through these lists before they appear anywhere else:

- **EarthScope mailing list** — InSAR courses, workshops, and announcements from the US geodesy community
- **CLaSH mailing list** — subduction zone early-career activities
- **ASF newsletter** — new HyP3 features and training events
- **COMET mailing list** — LiCSAR/LiCSBAS product updates and workshops
